About Us

Christian Foundation Fellowship Juja Farm is committed to TRUE WORSHIP as declared in John 4:24. Our mission and vision are centered on raising believers who worship in spirit and in truth, impacting the community through faith, love, and kingdom service.

Our Ministries

  • Youth Ministry - Empowering young believers in faith.
  • Worship Team - Leading the congregation into true worship.
  • Women's Ministry - Strengthening women in Christ.
  • Sunday School - Teaching children biblical foundations.
  • Kingdom Men - Building strong, godly leaders.
